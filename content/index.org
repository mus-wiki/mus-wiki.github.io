** About this site
*** Ethics
MUS-Wiki page is designed to adhere to the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacker_ethic][hacker ethic]], with the most important rules being:
+ *All information should be free*
+ *Hackers should be judged by their hacking, not bogus criteria such as degrees, age, race, sex, or position*
+ *Computers can change your life for the better*

/Hacking does NOT refer to criminal activities, but in playfully doing something difficult, useful or not/

This site purpose is to help you hack your way into the medical field, and to help others along your journey.

Contributions are always welcomed.
